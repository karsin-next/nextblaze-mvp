## Avoid These Mistakes
- **Being too vague:** "People want better apps." (Bad)
- **Focusing on features:** "Our app has a faster database." (Bad - move this to the Solution slide)
- **Lack of data:** No evidence that the problem is widespread.

> [!TIP]
> Use a real-world example or a customer quote to make the problem feel "human" and relatable.
